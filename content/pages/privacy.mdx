# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 25, 2024**

## Introduction

StructIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-based civil plan generation and project management platform.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and contact details
- Professional credentials and certifications
- Company affiliation and role
- Login credentials
- Payment information

### Project Data
- Building plans and specifications
- Project reports and documentation
- Team member assignments and roles
- Project timelines and milestones
- Construction site data and measurements

### Usage Data
- Platform interaction patterns
- AI model usage statistics
- Feature preferences
- Device and browser information
- IP address and location data

## How We Use Your Information

- Generate and optimize civil engineering plans
- Create and manage project reports
- Track project progress and team performance
- Improve our AI models and algorithms
- Enhance platform features and user experience
- Process payments and maintain accounts
- Communicate important updates and notifications

## Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive information
- Regular security audits and updates
- Secure cloud storage with redundant backups
- Access controls and authentication protocols
- Compliance with engineering industry standards

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with:
- Project team members (based on permissions)
- Third-party service providers for platform operations
- Legal authorities when required by law
- Professional bodies for certification verification

We do not sell your personal information or project data to third parties.

## AI Model Training

Our AI models are trained on:
- Anonymized project data
- Public domain construction standards
- Industry best practices
- User interaction patterns

You can opt out of contributing to AI model training while maintaining full platform functionality.

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request data deletion
- Export project data
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Control data sharing settings

## Data Retention

We retain your information for:
- Active accounts: Duration of account activity
- Completed projects: 7 years (regulatory compliance)
- Inactive accounts: 2 years before deletion
- Payment records: As required by law

## Children's Privacy

StructIQ is designed for professional use and does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or platform notifications.
